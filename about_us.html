Welcome to LandShark! CD Duplication & Design, Inc.

Taking care of all of your CD & DVD duplicating needs


LandShark was founded in the Fall of 2003 by Craig Palanker and Kris Landherr with the goal of helping the independent artist put out a great looking CD or DVD at an affordable price. Since then we have gained a loyal following from corporate clients and independent artists alike. Each client is provided individual attention from our staff of graphic designers, audio engineers and video technicians. You will be provided with a complete mock-up of your product for your approval. You can order the exact number of CD's or DVD's that you need without being forced to order hundreds more than you actually need. With attention to detail, quick turnaround and friendly service, we have won over many fans. In a short time, LandShark's reputation for excellence has grown among musical artists in every genre from Cabaret to Hip-Hop.

We've designed artwork, edited and authored CD's and DVD's for music, training, promotional and documentary videos including Eric Bogsian, Kiki and Herb's "Total Eclipse of the Heart," PBS's "In the Life," The National Automotive Technology Competition, Nets Basketball and Rome Neal's acclaimed production "Monk."

LandShark is committed to giving you a product to be proud of; one that shows off your talent and individuality and best gets your message out to the world.
